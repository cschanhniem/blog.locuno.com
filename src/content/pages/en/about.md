Locuno AI Handbook is the editorial space behind Locuno Academy. It is written for parents, learners, and anyone trying to think clearly about what childhood, learning, and digital maturity now mean in an AI-shaped world. We are not interested in hype for its own sake. We care about how children learn, what habits they build, and which boundaries help them grow with technology instead of being consumed by it.

The publication is organized around three working tracks: education, digital skills, and safety. We write about foundations before shortcuts, judgment before automation, and the practical routines families can adopt before handing a child a device, an account, or an AI assistant.

If Locuno Academy builds the pathway, Locuno AI Handbook keeps the conversation honest. It gives families language for hard questions, structure for better decisions, and a steadier way to meet a future that is arriving faster than most schools can explain.
